About

My name is Hock Kim. I'm an independent professional providing interim management and consulting services.

I spent my career in operational and consulting roles starting up manufacturing plants for small and large companies and helping them improve operations. The challenges I liked best were project-based work: bringing clarity to ambiguous problems, building organizational capabilities, and executing operational improvements, training and coaching, and change management.

I founded Bauen Bridge Pte Ltd as a solo practice to focus my energy on these topics and offer my expertise to different clients with minimal fuss. Working with a solo practice means what you see is precisely what you get. We will work directly with each other. There are no junior employees to be staffed and no business partners to negotiate with. Keeping things simple typically leads to satisfying outcomes.

What We Do

I help your team build capabilities and improve operations.

Project execution and operations consulting

In periods of change or crisis, you may not have enough management resources or suitable skills in your organization to handle the issue or opportunity at hand. And a permanent position to address it may not be necessary or could be difficult to fill on short notice.

Therefore, you want, on a temporary basis, an experienced professional to size up the situation quickly, provide constructive inputs, develop a plan with your organization and execute the change.

How We Work

We can work on a consulting project basis or an interim management arrangement.

In our initial contact, we make a preliminary assessment together of what you need and if I am able to help. If we decide that there is a fit, we make a provisional engagement for a diagnosis where I research and form a detailed understanding of the situation and propose possible approaches. At this point, the proposal may differ from our preliminary assessment and therefore we review your projected return on investment to proceed with the engagement. Our engagement may end here if the ROI is not sensible.

If we decide to proceed, I will develop an action plan with various stakeholders from your organization and execute with full accountability for the results. At the end of the engagement, we will review the results against our objectives and check that knowledge is handed over to your organization.

Then we pat ourselves on the back, shake hands, and part ways. And I look forward to serve you again in a different situation.

Training and capability building

Training is a means to an end. Yet too often training is organized as an event to 'check a box' with cookie-cutter modules picked off a menu. And the ROI on the time and money spent is forgotten in the process. You have specific objectives and your team has specific needs. Effective training should be customized to fit both. Therefore, the same diagnostic and results-oriented approach will be applied to help you clarify the training objectives and achieve them.
